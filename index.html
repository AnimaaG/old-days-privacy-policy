Privacy Policy

for the mobile game Old Days

Last updated: 2025-08-14

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Old Days (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our game.

1. Information We Collect

  • Device and app info: device model, OS version, language, country, app version, and crash logs.
  • Identifiers: advertising identifier (e.g., Google Advertising ID) used for ads frequency-capping and analytics.
  • Gameplay and usage data: session length, screens visited, levels completed, and generic events to improve the game.

No account is required. We do not knowingly collect contact information such as name, email, phone number, or precise location.

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate, maintain, and improve gameplay and performance.
  • Measure the effectiveness of features and fix bugs and crashes.
  • Show non‑intrusive ads and limit repetitive ads where applicable.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent misuse.

3. Third‑Party SDKs & Sharing

We may share limited, pseudonymous data with service providers who help us operate the game, for example:

  • Advertising networks (to display ads and measure performance).
  • Analytics/crash reporting (to understand usage and stability).

These partners process data on our behalf under their privacy terms. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Children’s Privacy

We are committed to protecting children’s privacy. If your child is under the age of 13 (or the age required by your region), the game is intended to limit data to that necessary to provide the experience and to use only child‑appropriate services where applicable. If you believe a child has provided personal data to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate action, including deletion.

5. Data Retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, after which it is deleted or anonymized.

6. Your Choices & Rights

  • You can reset your device’s Advertising ID or limit ad tracking in your device settings.
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of data processed about you by contacting us at the email below.

7. International Transfers

Your information may be processed on servers outside your country. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

8. Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect information from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The updated Policy will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
